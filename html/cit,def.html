Avatar, marketed as James Cameron's Avatar, is a 2009 American epic science fiction film directed,written,produced,and co-edited by James Cameron, and stars Sam Worthington,Zoe Saldana,Stephen Lang,Michelle Rodriguez, and Sigourney Weaver. The film is set in the mid-22nd century, when humans are colonizing Pandora,a lush habitable moon of a giant in the Alpha Centauri star system, in order to mine the mineral onabtanium, a room-temperature superconductor. The expansion of the mining colony threatens the continued existence of a local tribe of Navi a humanoid species indegenous to Pandora. The film's title genetically engineered Navi body with the mind of a remotely located human that is intercat with the natives of Pandora.

Avatar derives from a sanskrit word "descent"and when it first appeared in English in the late 18th century, it referred to the descent of a deity to the earth - typically, the incarnation in earthly form of vishnu or another Hindu deity. It later came to refer to any incarnation in human form, and then to any embodiment (such as that of a concept or philosophy), whether or not in the form of a person. In the age of tecnology "Avatar" has developed another sense - it can now be used for the image that a person chooses as his or her "embodiment" in an electric medium.
